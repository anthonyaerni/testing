Priority Recommendations (1-4)

Critical actions to accelerate maturity

1. Formalize Governance & Customer Intelligence Strategy

Critical

Action: Establish cross-functional CI steering committee with Product Operations, Product Leadership, Engineering, Support, and Sales representatives.

Outcome: Formal CI strategy aligned with 2026 North Star (Product Quality) and strategic priorities: JTBD, Hosting & AI Adoption, WooCommerce Simplicity. Define quarterly OKRs.

Timeline: Q1 2026 (0-3 months)

2. Build ROI Model Connecting CI to Product Quality

Critical

Action: Develop quantitative model showing correlation between CI insights and Product Quality metrics (active bugs, point releases, merchant-reported issues).

Outcome: Executive dashboard showing how CI drives Product Quality improvements, enables data-driven bug fix prioritization, reduces engineering rework.

Timeline: Q1 2026 (0-3 months)

3. Map Customer Intelligence to JTBD Framework

High Priority

Action: Analyze merchant feedback through JTBD lens for core journeys (order fulfillment, setup flows, payments, analytics, tax, shipping).

Outcome: Prioritized journey improvements aligned with merchant jobs. Connect feedback themes to JTBD categories for roadmap planning.

Timeline: Q1-Q2 2026 (0-6 months)

4. Implement Systematic Closed-Loop Processes

High Priority

Action: Design inner loop (1:1 merchant follow-up) and outer loop (cross-functional improvements) using Enterpret workflows and Slack integrations.

Outcome: Reduce response time to critical issues, create customer-centric culture, document root causes for systemic Product Quality improvements.

Timeline: Q1-Q2 2026 (0-6 months)
